# How to Choose a Responsible Golden Retriever Breeder

Golden Retrievers are wonderful family dogs, but starting with a healthy puppy from an ethical breeder is key.

## Red Flags to Avoid
- Breeders who always have puppies available
- No health testing on parents

## What to Look For
- OFA hip/elbow certification
- Eye clearance
- Heart testing
