# Common Health Issues in Golden Retrievers

Goldens are loving but prone to some genetic conditions – early awareness helps.

## Hip and Elbow Dysplasia
- Symptoms: Limping, reluctance to exercise
- Prevention: Choose breeders with OFA certification (see our [Breeder Directory](/breeders))

## Cancer Risk
- Goldens have higher rates – regular checkups essential
